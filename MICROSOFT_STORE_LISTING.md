### Overview
Create professional identification cards in minutes with Universal ID Card Generator. Perfect for corporations, educational institutions, government agencies, and event management companies. Our enterprise-grade platform combines intuitive design with advanced security features to deliver authentic ID cards instantly.

### What You Can Do
**Generate Professional ID Cards**
- Choose from 6 premium, professionally designed templates
- Customize colors, fonts, layouts, and branding
- Add logos, photos, and background images
- Apply watermarks for additional security

**Support Global Organizations**
- Select from 250+ countries for nationality designation
- Perfect for international teams and distributed workforces
- Support multiple languages and date formats

**Ensure Card Authenticity**
- Embedded QR codes for instant verification
- Machine Readable Zone (MRZ) with international standards
- Unique ID number generation
- Digital signature support

**Verify Cards Instantly**
- Search by ID number to verify card status
- Scan QR codes for quick authentication
- Real-time authenticity confirmation
- View cardholder information securely

**Export Flexibly**
- PNG format for digital sharing and display
- PDF format for printing and archiving
- High-resolution output suitable for professional printing
- Batch export capabilities

**Manage Card Lifecycle**
- Track card status (Valid, Expired, Revoked)
- Automatic expiry date tracking
- Revoke cards instantly
- Complete audit history
